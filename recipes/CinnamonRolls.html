Cinnamon Rolls

Description

The fluffiest cinnamon rolls with a cinnamon laced center and a tangy creamy frosting to pair with them.

Ingredients

Dough

  • 3.25 oz sugar
  • .5 tsp salt
  • 3 Oz butter
  • 1 egg
  • Zest of one lemon
  • 16 oz bread flour
  • 1 packet of instant yeast
  • 8 oz butter milk

Filling

  • .5 c sugar
  • 1.5 T cinnamon
  • .25 c brown sugar

Cream Cheese frosting

  • 2 c confectioners sugar
  • 8 oz cream cheese
  • 4 oz butter
  • Splash of vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Add in sugar, room temp butter, and salt and with a paddle attachment, cream
  2. Add in egg and zest, mix until ribbon-like texture
  3. Add in flour, yeast, buttermilk, mix until just combined
  4. Swap attachment with a dough hook
  5. Mix until gluten has developed
  6. Proof in an oiled bowl for 2 hrs, until doubled in size
  7. Place on oiled surface, roll out 14x18 inch
  8. Combine the ingredients for the filling together and spread filling across rolled out dough
  9. Tightly roll up the dough
  10. Discard the ends
  11. Cut into 12 equal pieces
  12. Place in casserole dish with .5 inch space between each roll
  13. Proof for 90 mins
  14. Glaze with melted butter before baking
  15. Bake at 375F for 25-30 mins, until internal temperature reaches 190F
  16. Sift confectioners sugar into mixing bowl
  17. Add in room temperature cream cheese and butter
  18. Add in vanilla extract
  19. Thin out with HC/milk, until it resembles drape like texture
  20. While cinnamon buns are still warm, drape over them
